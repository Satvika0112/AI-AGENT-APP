# Churn Risk Playbook

## Signals that a customer may churn
- Usage declining sharply (e.g. more than 30% drop over 60 days)
- Health score below 45
- A champion or key sponsor has left the company
- Customer explicitly mentions evaluating competitors or budget pressure
- Low seat activation (few of the paid seats are actually used)

## Recommended plays
- **Champion left:** Build a relationship with a new champion quickly. For high-value accounts, escalate to a senior CSM or exec sponsor.
- **Usage dropping before renewal:** Schedule a value-review call before the renewal date. Re-establish the business outcomes the customer cares about.
- **Considering competitors:** Acknowledge it directly, quantify the value already delivered, and bring in an exec sponsor. Do not wait.
- Always tie the conversation to measurable outcomes, not features.
