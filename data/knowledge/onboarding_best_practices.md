# Onboarding & Adoption Best Practices

## Why adoption stalls
- Initial setup (first project / first workflow) was never completed
- Only a few seats activated; the wider team never started
- No clear "first win" was defined for the customer

## What to do
- Run a guided setup session to complete the first project together.
- Drive seat activation: find inactive users and invite them with a specific task.
- Share the relevant quick-start guide and define one concrete early win.
- For low-adoption accounts, enablement/training usually beats a sales motion.
