# Product Overview & Plans

## Plans
- **Starter** - up to 10 seats, core features, email support.
- **Business** - up to 100 seats, automations, integrations, priority support.
- **Enterprise** - unlimited seats, custom dashboards, advanced analytics, data export, SSO, dedicated CSM.

## Notable features by tier
- Custom dashboards: Enterprise only
- Advanced analytics & data export: Enterprise only
- Automations & integrations: Business and above
- Guided onboarding / setup wizard: all plans
