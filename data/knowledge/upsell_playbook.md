# Upsell & Expansion Playbook

## When to consider an upsell
- Health score above 70 and usage trending up
- Customer is hitting the limits of their current plan (seats or features)
- Power users requesting capabilities available on a higher tier
- The team is growing

## How to approach it
- Lead with the customer's expressed need, not the upgrade.
- If they ask about a feature that lives on a higher tier, connect that feature to their goal first, then mention the plan.
- Bundle additional seats into the upgrade conversation when the team is growing.
- Send concrete pricing promptly while interest is high.
